Krabby Patty

Yummy Yummy Krabby Patty

To make a Krabby Patty, start by combining 1 lb of ground beef with 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs, 1/4 cup of finely chopped onion, 1 tsp of garlic powder, 1/2 tsp of salt, and 1/4 tsp of black pepper. Form the mixture into 4 patties, ensuring they’re even in size. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side until they're browned and fully cooked through.

Once the patties are cooked, place them on a bun and add your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese, and a dollop of ketchup or mustard. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious, homemade Krabby Patty!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Toppings: lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese, ketchup, mustard (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty mix: In a bowl, combine 1 lb of ground beef, 1/4 cup breadcrumbs, 1/4 cup finely chopped onion, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Form the patties: Shape the mixture into 4 equal-sized patties, pressing gently to ensure they’re even.
  3. Cook the patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they’re browned and fully cooked through.
