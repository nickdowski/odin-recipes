Yummy Yummy Chicken Wings

For crispy chicken wings, preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Pat 2 lbs of chicken wings dry, then toss them in olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper (optional), and baking powder for extra crispiness. Arrange the wings in a single layer on the sheet or a wire rack and bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.

Once the wings are done, toss them in your favorite sauce like buffalo, BBQ, or honey garlic if desired. Serve immediately with dipping sauce on the side and enjoy your crispy, flavorful wings!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Sauce (optional): Buffalo, BBQ, Honey Garlic, or Sexy Redd's Sexy Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven: Set your oven to 400째F (200째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare the wings: If the wings are whole, separate them into drumettes and flats by cutting through the joint.
  3. Dry the wings: Pat the wings dry with paper towels. This step is key to getting them crispy!
  4. Season: In a large bowl, toss the wings with olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, pepper, cayenne (if using), and baking powder. Make sure the wings are well coated.
  5. Arrange on baking sheet: Place the wings in a single layer on the baking sheet. For extra crispiness, use a wire rack over the baking sheet.
  6. Bake: Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the wings are golden and crispy.
  7. Sauce (optional): If you want to coat the wings with sauce, toss them in your favorite sauce (like buffalo, BBQ, or honey garlic) after they come out of the oven.
  8. Serve: Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!
